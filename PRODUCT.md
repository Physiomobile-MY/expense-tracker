# Product Brief: ExpenseFlow

## One-Line Pitch

Expense claim workflow for SMEs and clinics, with receipt upload, AI extraction, duplicate detection, approval routing, finance notifications, reports, and audit logs.

## Target Customer

- Clinics and healthcare groups
- SMEs with staff expense claims
- Teams currently using WhatsApp, Google Forms, Excel, or manual receipt folders
- Finance teams needing simple approval and reimbursement control

## Pain Solved

Expense claims are scattered, receipts go missing, approvals are unclear, and finance teams waste time retyping receipt details. ExpenseFlow centralizes submission, extraction, approval, audit, and reporting.

## Core Workflows

- Staff uploads receipt and claim details
- AI extracts merchant, date, amount, and category
- System checks duplicates and policy flags
- Manager approves or rejects
- Finance receives notification and prepares reimbursement
- Admin reviews reports, AI logs, and audit logs

## Commercial Packaging

Product name idea: ExpenseFlow

Suggested pricing:

- Setup: RM500 to RM2,000
- Starter: RM49/month
- Business: RM99/month
- Pro: RM199/month with higher claim volume, multiple departments, and export workflows
